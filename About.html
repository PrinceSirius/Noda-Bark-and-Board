About NoDa Bark and board

Bark and Board is conveniently located two miles north of Uptown Charlotte in NoDa, Charlotte's Historic Arts District. Our facility includes over 30,000 square feet of indoor and outdoor play space for doggie daycare and large dog suites for comfy overnight boarding. Our facility is climate controlled and contains fire and security protection.

Our mission at Bark and Board is to provide a safe, clean and fun atmosphere for your furry friend to romp, relax and/or sleepover. We recognize that a tired dog is a happy, well-behaved dog, and we guarantee that your dog will be tuckered out by the end of the day.

DOGGIE DAY CARE INCLUDES: Supervised doggie playgroups organized by age, size and temperament Indoor/outdoor morning and afternoon play sessions 2 hour nap time Administration of any medications Adherence to special dietary needs

OVERNIGHT BOARDING INCLUDES: All of the above listed in Doggie Day Care Comfy dog cot - no sleeping on concrete floors! Bowls - you only need to pack food Evening relief and tuck-in
